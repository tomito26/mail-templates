Password Reset Disclaimer

For your security, please be aware of the following:

  1. OTP Validity: The One-Time Password (OTP) you receive for password reset is valid for a limited time. Please use it promptly.
  2. Email Verification: Ensure that the email containing the OTP originates from workforcehr.app. Do not enter the OTP if you didn't request a password reset.
  3. Phishing Awareness: Be cautious of any unsolicited emails or messages asking for your OTP. WorkForce Africa will never ask for your OTP outside of the password reset process.
  4. Secure Environment: Reset your password only in a trusted and secure environment. Avoid using public or shared devices.
  5. Password Strength: Choose a strong, unique password that includes a mix of upper and lower case letters, numbers, and symbols. Do not use easily guessable information like birthdays or names.
  6. Immediate Change: Once you reset your password, log in to your account immediately to complete the process and secure your account.
